Admissions

Joining Wonder Kids means giving your child the gift of a strong foundation in academics, creativity, and values.

How to Apply

  1. Visit our school or contact us to request an admission form.
  2. Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  3. Schedule an interaction/assessment (where applicable).
  4. Receive admission confirmation and complete enrollment.

Requirements

  • Birth certificate of the child
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Previous school report (for transfer students)
